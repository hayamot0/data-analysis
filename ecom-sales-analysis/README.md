# E-Commerce Sales Analysis 2019

## About
Analysis of 185,000+ sales orders across 12 months for a US-based electronics 
e-commerce store, covering revenue trends, product performance, and customer behavior.

## Questions answered
- Which product sold the most units?
- Which month had the highest sales and revenue?
- Which city generates the most revenue?
- Total gross revenue for 2019
- Busiest hours of the day for orders
- Which product generates the most revenue?
- Average order value
- Top 5 products by revenue

## Key findings
- Highest revenue month: December
- Most sold product: USB-C Charging Cable
- Highest revenue product: Macbook Pro Laptop
- Busiest ordering hour: 12pm and 7pm
- Top city by revenue: San Francisco
