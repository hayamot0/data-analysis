# Restaurant Orders Analysis

## About
Analysis of 550 restaurant orders using Python and Pandas, covering revenue patterns, customer behavior, and operational insights.

## Questions answered
- Total revenue generated
- Most revenue by restaurant type
- Most ordered item
- Busiest day of the week
- Most popular payment method
- Top 5 customers by spending
- Percentage of orders with special requests
- Revenue by time of day

## Tools used
Python, Pandas, Matplotlib

## Dataset
Hotel Restaurant Orders dataset from Kaggle
