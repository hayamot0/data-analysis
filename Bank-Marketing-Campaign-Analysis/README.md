# Bank Marketing Campaign Analysis

## About
Analysis of 11,162 bank customers contacted during a telemarketing campaign 
to subscribe to a term deposit. Identifies key factors that influence 
customer subscription decisions.

## Questions answered
- Overall subscription rate
- Which age group subscribes most?
- Which job type has highest subscription rate?
- Which education level subscribes most?
- Do customers with loans subscribe less?
- Do homeowners subscribe more?
- Which contact method converts best?
- Does call duration affect subscription?
- Does previous campaign success predict future subscription?
